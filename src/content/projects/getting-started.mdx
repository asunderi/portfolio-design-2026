## Overview

A suite of external interfaces for a post-secondary education institution. The
work spanned research, design, and front-end build — translating institutional
requirements into accessible, on-brand templates inside a content management
system.

## Contributions

- Designed reusable page templates in Figma
- Built responsive front-end components with HTML and SCSS
- Integrated designs into the institution's CMS

## Outcome

A consistent, maintainable interface system that the institution's content
teams could extend without breaking the design language.
